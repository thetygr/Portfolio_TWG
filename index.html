01 · Flagship Innovation · Medical Device
● Provisional Patent Pending
Inercept™ — Active Counter-Torque Protection System
First active helmet technology · $7.4B TAM · Zero competitors · Techstars Founder Catalyst 2026 · CU Ventures EASE
The first wearable protective technology that actively generates counter-force opposing rotational head acceleration at the moment of impact. 87% of concussions and traumatic brain injuries result from rotational force — every existing helmet addresses only the remaining 13% via passive absorption alone. Inercept detects, computes, and responds sub-millisecond, before the brain has meaningfully displaced relative to the skull. Trademark cleared. Prior art gap confirmed. Go-to-market mirrors the MIPS ingredient-brand licensing model that reached a $1.2B market cap, targeting five domains: sports, military, industrial, aerospace, and medical rehabilitation.
